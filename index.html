About

Who is
aespa?

Aespa is a South Korean girl group from SM Entertainment with four members: Karina, Giselle, Winter, and Ningning. They debuted on November 17, 2020, with the song “Black Mamba.” The group is known for their unique concept that mixes real-life members with virtual characters, creating a futuristic story.

Aespa is popular for their strong performances, catchy songs, and modern style, making them one of the most well-known K-pop groups today.
